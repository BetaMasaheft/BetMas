Paratexts in Beta maṣāḥǝft

The project Beta maṣāḥǝft: Manuscripts of Ethiopia and Eritrea (Schriftkultur des christlichen Äthiopiens und Eritreas: eine multimediale Forschungsumgebung) pays particular attention to encoding paratextual information. Since the types of data are manifold, no unique search has so far been developed, but you can choose some of the options on the left to access our data.
