Available Digitized Manuscripts

This list aims at being a comprehensive resource for all available digitized Ethiopic manuscripts. This is only possible thanks to the contributions of many people. If you know about digitized manuscripts that are not listed here, or would like to correct anything, please contact us by opening an issue or by sending an e-mail to a team member of Beta maṣāḥəft. Thank you very much!
