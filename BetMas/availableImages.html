Available Digitized Manuscripts

This list aims at being a comprehensive resource for all available digitized Ethiopic manuscripts. This is only possible thanks to the contributions of many people. If you know about digitized manuscripts that are not listed here, or would like to correct anything, please contact us by opening an issue or by sending an e-mail to a team member of Beta maṣāḥəft. This list was inspired by and developed upon the Digitized Ethiopian manuscripts online list maintained by Ted Erho. Thank you very much!

  • The manuscripts images available here in Beta maṣāḥǝft, which include some of the manuscripts digitized by the Ethio-SpaRe project, some of those by the EMIP project and those of the Biblioteca Medicea Laurenziana. Please note that via our Mirador viewer we also link to manifests made available by other institutions like the Digital Vatican Library and the Bibliothèque nationale de France.
