The Fricke Collection

The Fricke collection is a small private collection of Gəʿəz manuscripts currently held in Hude, Germany. The five manuscripts – three Psalter codices and two scrolls – were acquired by Dr Eva Omansen (1917-1990) during her stay in Ethiopia (1963-1974), where she worked as a doctor at the Ḫayla Śǝllāse I Hospital in ʾAddis ʾAbabā. We are very grateful to Dr. Treeske Fricke, Eva Omansen’s daughter, and her family for making this collection available for scientific description and digitization.
